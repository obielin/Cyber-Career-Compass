# Privacy Notice (Cyber Career Compass)

## What data is collected
- Assessment responses (e.g., interests, experience level).
- Email address **only if the user opts to receive their report by email**.

## Purpose of collection
- Assessment responses are used to generate personalised cybersecurity pathway recommendations.
- Email addresses are used **solely to deliver the generated report** to the user.

## Where processing happens
- Assessment processing occurs in the browser.
- Email delivery is handled by the applicationâ€™s backend or email service provider (if configured).

## Data retention
- Email addresses are retained only for as long as necessary to deliver the report.
- No email addresses are used for marketing or ongoing communication.
- No profiling beyond report generation is performed.

## Data sharing
- Email addresses are not shared with third parties except where required to send the email (e.g., an email delivery service).

## User rights
- Users may request deletion of their data by opening a GitHub issue or contacting the maintainer.
- Requests will be handled within a reasonable timeframe.

## Contact
For privacy-related questions or deletion requests, please open a GitHub issue in this repository.
